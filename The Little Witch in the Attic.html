The Little Witch in the Attic

Puppet Character Design and Environment Development

Character Design


Rowan is a 13-year old girl with strong interest in witchcraft. She is timid and quirky, a loner at school. She likes wandering around in the town, picking waste as her witchcraft materials. She accidentally summoned a cat demon in her attic

Mafloga is a three-eye cat demon with a black flame burning all over its body except for its wounded ear. Because of Rowan's immature witchcraft, Mafloga is too weak to maintain its demon shape and has to transform into a black cat.






Environment Design


Rowan lives with her parents who run a bad business second-hand shop in a gloomy British town.
