Tetris Shoe Rack

A modular shoe storage organizer for shoes of various heights.

Brief

Tetris Shoe Rack is a modular shoe rack set that consists of blocks with different shapes and colors, designed to store shoes of various heights. Customers can assemble the blocks in different angles and directions for their space and preference.



Problem

Shoe storage problems include low space efficiency (when storing shoes of different heights), single function, limited storage capacity, room suitability, complexity of assembling, and difficulty in viewing and obtaining shoes.

Aesthetic

Transparent materials not only make it easier to view and obtain shoes but also make assembling more fun and aesthetic: stacking up two blocks of different colors can create a new color on the contact surface.



Multipurpose

The shoe rack functions as a shoe bench as well. The neutral and simple design makes it a storage rack with a lot of versatility that may also work as a book or plant shelf.
