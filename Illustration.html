Uncanny


This series uncovers the dark side of Asian culture often disregarded or misinterpreted by westerners. The subject of each piece is spiritual rituals from different Asian cultures: Manchu Shaman Dancing, the Chinese Ghost Marriage, and the Thai Kuman Thong. The exotic elements revealed in light are always the focus, while the uncanny parts are hidden in shadow: animal sacrifice in Shaman Dance, the paper puppet representing the dead in Ghost Marriage, and the statue of a deceased child being worshiped in Kuman Thong. The texts are in the traditional languages of each culture.
