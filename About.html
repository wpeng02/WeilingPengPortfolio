About Me

I’m Weiling Peng, a designer and illustrator from Nanjing, China. I’m currently pursuing a BFA in Industrial Design and Illustration at the Rhode Island School of Design. My areas of interest include play product design and concept art.


I believe in the power of play. It makes possible a level of engagement which in turn makes possible the experience of flow, that heightened sense of being. As a gamer, I most enjoy the small delightful moments in my play experience: figuring out a clever way to accomplish a difficult task, gaining a spectacular view when I climb to the highest point, experiencing a thought-provoking moment in the narrative, and noticing an “EasterEgg” design detail. So as a designer, I set out to create experiences of heightened play, creating moments of surprise and delight for users.
