Story of Liber-Land Lion Park

Character Design and Story Development

Summary


This is a two-person collaborative project of a series of character designs with background story writings. The story is inspired by the Taigan Lion park located in Crimea. We wish to bring attention to wildlife rescue and protection through the story.


(Team member: Audrey Chen)

Story Brief


The story is about an old man Denis building a wildlife park, Liber-land Lion Park, for the rescued lions. The story has five chapters, each unfolding from the perspective of a lion character who is rescued by Denis and the veterinarian Jas to the lion park. As the story progresses, the zoo gradually becomes a paradise for wildlife.



Character Design

Denis

Denis is a retired air force Lieutenant. He is an old man with a serious-looking face but a kind heart caring for animals.



Background Story: During Dennis's service, he once attended a battle in which he parachuted to a grassland deviated from the original landing point. He encountered a wild lion pride and miraculously formed a friendship with the lions. However, the war spread and the lions suffered heavy casualties. Feeling extremely guilty, Denis retired after the war and returned to the former military base which was abandoned. He found the young lion king Sargon and his pack, who might be the descendent of the previous lion pack. The group was struggling to live in the destroyed grassland, so Denis spent his life fortune to buy this place and began to build a safe place for the lions to live.

Jas

Jas is a rebellious punk girl who's a walking catnip loved by every lion in the park.



Background Story: Jas was born into a wealthy family. She always wanted to have a pet in her childhood, but her father was adamantly against it and even poisoned the stray cats wandering around their villa in the food Jaz fed. Jaz was furious and left home to study to become a veterinarian. After graduating from college, Jaz accidentally saw the zoo veterinary recruitment posted by Denis, so she moved to the zoo that was still under construction. Denis is the opposite of Jas's father, and his silent concern for the lions and Jaz makes her feel warm. The zoo gradually becomes the home of Jaz, and Denis is like a father figure to her.

Sargon is a serious imperatorial character. As the previous and the first lion king of the pack, he built and ruled his kingdom through many battles. He didn't trust Denis at first, but after he noticed that Denis was rebuilding the damaged environment, providing food to the pack, and brought the veterinarian Jas to treat the injured lions. He gradually developed trust and friendship with Denis and Jas.

Aaron is an outgoing, attractive, and a little flirty character, who loves dancing to music. He was saved by Jas from a lion hunting group. On the way back to the lion park, Aaron listened to punk music and jazz genre in Jas’s truck for days and developed his interest in music and dancing.

Blob is skinny, timid, and always looks sad. He was rescued from the Youtube influencer who tortured animals behind the camera. It took him a while to get used to his new life in the lion park with help from Denis and Jas.

Rei is a beloved king who rules with mercy and justice. He is the apprentice and successor of the old king but with different ruling measures.

Lexi is a strong grumpy lioness and a good fighter. She was rescued from the circus in which she was forced to perform acrobatics and fight other animals in fighting shows. She used to be very aggressive and short-tempered but later was accepted by the pride with the help of Denis and Jas.

Nikolai is Sargon’s son, beloved by everyone as the little prince. He has innocent eyes but is really good at getting into mischief that annoys the pack members. Nikolai wants to prove his power to the next king by trying to frequently challenge the current lion king Rei but fails every time.

The Local Government Officer


The local government officer is a greedy man who tries to purchase the lion park from Denis and turn it into a private hunting park for upper government authorities.

The Youtube Influencer


The influencer likes to show off his caged wild animals on his channel. He shows his “love” in the live streaming but mistreats them behind the camera. When lions are not a trend anymore, he immediately abandons Blob.

The Circus Trainer


To force big cats in the circus to perform, the cruel circus trainer abuses them with whips and electric shock, and fed them poorly cooked food.







Project Development


We planned to transfer the lion characters into toys. Due to time constraints, we completed the toy design and modeling of two lions.
