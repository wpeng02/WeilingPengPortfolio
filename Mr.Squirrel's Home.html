Mr. Squirrel's Home

Character and Environment Design for a Puppet and a Dollhouse

Character Design


Mr. Squirrel is an artist who lives alone and always works at home, making a living by selling his paintings and specimens of insects and plants he made. His daughter is an explorer traveling around the world who occasionally visits him. Mr. Squirrel loves his job. He enjoys cooking, gardening, and basking in the sun.





Environment Design


I wanted to craft a small space that is warm and safe, providing comfort and solitude for Mr. Squirrel to live and work in.
